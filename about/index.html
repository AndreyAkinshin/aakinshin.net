About

Andrey Akinshin

andrey.akinshin@gmail.com

Andrey Akinshin is a software developer and a mathematician, an author of blog posts and papers about statistics and programming, a conference speaker. He is a PhD, a performance engineer at JetBrains, the project lead of BenchmarkDotNet, the author of Pro .NET Benchmarking. Previously, he was a postdoc at the Weizmann Institute of Science, a Microsoft .NET MVP, an ACM ICPC medalist.
